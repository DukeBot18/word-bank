How To Play

Game Overview

  • Guess the Phrase of the day!
  • You have $1000 to start.
  • 2 free guesses!
  • Purchase Letters, Hints, and more Guesses!
  • Fill in the entire phrase and don't run out of money...
  • Go!

Purchases

  • Letters - Cost displayed on each letter
  • Hint - $150
  • Additional Guesses - $150
